FAQ

Common
questions

Still on the fence? Here are the answers to the questions everyone's thinking.

Ask us directly →

Who can attend Confluence 2026?

+

Confluence is open to students, aspiring entrepreneurs, early-career professionals (0–5 years) and creators who are serious about personal and professional growth. We look for intent and ambition, not a specific background or degree.

Is Confluence a residential event?

+

Confluence is a non-residential event. Sessions are held across three premium venues in Hyderabad between July 6-10. Participants are responsible for their own accommodation and travel. We're happy to provide venue details and nearby stay recommendations.

What does the registration fee cover?

+

Your registration covers full 5-day access to all sessions and workshops, the Concept Cocoon experience on Day 4, the Corporate Gala at Shilpakala Vedika on Day 5, all mentor interaction sessions, YANC community membership and your certificate of completion.

Do I need an idea or startup to participate in the Concept Cocoon?

+

Yes, you need a startup idea to participate in Concept Cocoon.

How many seats are available?

+

Confluence is a curated cohort seats are limited to ensure quality interactions and meaningful networking. We strongly recommend registering early to secure your spot. Early registrations also receive priority access to specific mentor sessions.

Will I receive a certificate?

+

Yes. All participants who complete the 5-day Confluence experience will receive a certificate of completion from YANC. This can be added to your LinkedIn profile and resume.

How do I contact YANC for more information?

+

Reach us at connect@yanc.in or call +91 6309 545 559. You can also follow @yancclub on social media or visit yanc.in for the latest updates.
