Passes & Pricing

Confluence 2026 offers four distinct pass types. Each pass grants access only to the specific sessions or events listed under that pass. Access is not interchangeable between pass types unless you hold the All Access Pass.

Pass 01 · Skill Track
Learning Lab Pass — ₹4,500 per person
3-day immersive workshop · Limited to 50 participants · Includes workshops, mentor access, certification, goodies kit, and snacks + lunch across 3 days.
Pass 02 · Startup Track
Concept Cocoon Pass — ₹1,000 per startup/team (registration fee)
Shark-tank-style pitch competition · Registration fee of ₹1,000 is payable upfront. Shortlisted teams will be required to pay an additional ₹4,000 pitching fee. Assured-funding recipients must become YANC members to avail the funding benefit.
Pass 03 · Network Track
Networking Gala Pass — ₹250 per person
Single evening event · Limited seating on a first-come, first-served basis. Includes welcome snacks, welcome gift, and networking access. Does not grant access to workshops, concept cocoon or networking dinner.
Pass 04 · Full Experience
All Access Pass — ₹6,000 per person
5-day full event access · Limited participants. Includes the complete Learning Lab (3 days), Concept Cocoon participation, Networking Gala Dinner, all certifications, mentor access, meals, goodies, and priority placement consideration.

All prices are listed in Indian Rupees (INR) and are inclusive of all applicable taxes including GST at 18%. No additional charges will be levied at the point of registration.

All Access Pass

  • The All Access Pass is limited to 100 participants and is subject to a more detailed application review process. YANC reserves the right to decline applications that do not meet its curation criteria.
