Learning Lab

A three-day immersive workshop designed to develop the next generation of leaders. Participants engage in hands-on sessions, guided mentorship, and real-world challenges curated by industry professionals.

3 Days · Individual

What's Included
  • Letter of Recommendation (LOR) from industry leaders & young leadership representatives

  • Internship opportunities — allocated based on individual performance metrics assessed during the workshop

  • Awards and recognition for outstanding participants

Pass 02 · Shark Tank

Concept Cocoon

A high-stakes, realistic shark-tank-style startup pitch competition where teams present their ventures to a distinguished panel of venture capitalists, angel investors, and seasoned entrepreneurs. Real-world exposure and actionable feedback for early-stage startups.

Competition Day · Per Startup / Team
₹1,000
per startup / team
02
What's Included
  • Mentorship access and funding opportunities — awarded based on the quality of the startup and selection by the panel
  • Official certification for all participating teams
  • Awards and recognition for winning and shortlisted teams
  • Curated, detailed feedback from VCs, angel investors, and lead entrepreneurs
  • Curated goodies kit per team
  • Snacks provided on the competition day
Important Notes
  • The ₹1,000 fee is for registration. Shortlisted teams will pay an additional ₹4,000 for pitching. Teams receiving assured funding must become YANC members to avail funding.
  • Teams must register with a complete startup profile before the deadline to confirm their slot.
  • 2 team members must be present on the day of the pitch.
Per startup / team · Competition day only

Policies

Good to Know

🚫
Non-Refundable

All tickets are non-refundable once issued. Please confirm your dates and availability before completing your registration.

🔒
Non-Transferable

Tickets are issued to individuals or teams and are non-transferable unless explicitly stated otherwise in your ticket terms.

⭐
Performance-Based Benefits

LOR issuance, internship placements, mentorship access, and funding opportunities are merit-based. They are not guaranteed for all participants — they are earned through performance and panel selection.

🪑
Limited Seats · Gala

The Networking Gala operates on a first-come, first-served basis. Seating is limited — early registration is strongly advised to secure your spot at the evening event.

📋
Startup Registration

For Concept Cocoon, teams must register with a complete startup profile before the registration deadline. Incomplete profiles will not be considered for slot confirmation.

✅
Taxes Included

All listed prices are fully inclusive of applicable taxes. No additional charges will be levied at the point of registration.
