Good to Know

🚫
Non-Refundable

All tickets are non-refundable once issued. Please confirm your dates and availability before completing your registration.

Non-Transferable

Tickets are issued to individuals or teams and are non-transferable unless explicitly stated otherwise in your ticket terms.

LOR issuance, internship placements, mentorship access, and funding opportunities are merit-based. They are not guaranteed for all participants — they are earned through performance and panel selection.

All listed prices are fully inclusive of applicable taxes. No additional charges will be levied at the point of registration.
